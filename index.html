  • A craft fair to showcase the talented artists and creatives in our schcool. It would be a great networking experience, we could invite local businesses and hold a raffle giving part of the proceeds to a charity NWRC would like to help
  • Concerts that the music and other students alike could take part in, given all the talent in NWRC. Again we could sell tickets and give part of the proceeds to charity, with the rest going to participants and to fund other college events.
